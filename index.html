SM Associates

S M ASSOCIATES is a contracting and consultancy firm to facilitate and provide reliable service to our Clients. We have all the machineries and equipment to carry out specialized contracting jobs. Our vast experience in application of construction chemicals, paints and specialized coating combined with our infrastructure and good technical team make our grounds strong in the competitive market.

We also undertake specialized contracting related to structural repair and rehabilitation like micro concreting, carbon fibre wrapping, epoxy flooring, cementitious flooring, civil redress and application of specialty coatings like anti-corrosive treatment, anti-carbonation coatings and ultra-chemical resistant coatings and other coating applications.

Our Mission is to educate the customers the uses and advantages of construction chemicals and use them in a cost-effective way. To add value to clients through innovations, reliability, exceptional quality services and faster an eco-friendly environment and pursuing accurate application practices.
